About



In Spring 2020, I was ready for a career change after 20 + years in Retirement & Investments Operations. I was not sure how my new career would look like, but I decided to take a leap into Technology. I was so excited to be offered an opportunity to attend the TechBridge Technology Career Program.

I am amazed how quickly I learned and applied new skills: from building websites with HTML, CSS, Javascript to sharing codes and pushing them on GitHub.

It is in my nature to persevere and to work things through the end, but this program pushed and tested me day after day. I love how it stretches me beyond what I thought I could accomplish. As I chart my course into ServiceNow, I am excited about how I can combine my experience in Customer Service, HR, Risk Management with Technology.
